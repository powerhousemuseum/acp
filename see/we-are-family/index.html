We Are Family

We Are Family

Michele Aboud | Deborah Kelly | Annie Magdalena Laerkesen | r e a | The Twilight Girls | Waded. 

Curated by C. Moore Hardy 

1 March – 18 May 2014 

We Are Family challenges the definition of family by looking at the notion within gay, lesbian, bisexual, transgender, intersex and queer (GLBTIQ) contexts. With humour, sensitivity, wit and sometimes anger, the artists invite the audience to consider the personal and the human beyond the political.
